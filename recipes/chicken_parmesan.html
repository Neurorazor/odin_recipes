Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

This chicken parmesan recipe is a classic Italian-American dish made with breaded chicken cutlets, tomato sauce, and melted cheese. It's a filling and satisfying meal that's sure to impress your dinner guests. Serve it with a side of pasta or a salad for a complete and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Pound the chicken breasts until they are evenly thick, about 1/2 inch.
  3. Season the flour with salt and pepper. Coat the chicken in the seasoned flour, then dip in the beaten eggs, and finally coat in the breadcrumb and Parmesan mixture.
  4. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Spread a layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Place the cooked chicken in the dish and top with more sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite side dish.
