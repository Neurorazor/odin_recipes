Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese filling. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for feeding a crowd or for leftovers throughout the week. With a blend of spices and flavorful ingredients, this lasagna will quickly become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, cook beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine mozzarella cheese, cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs.
  4. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  5. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer half of the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture. Repeat layers.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  7. Uncover and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted and bubbly.
