Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This spaghetti recipe is a classic Italian dish made with al dente pasta, a rich tomato sauce, and flavorful herbs and spices. It's a simple and satisfying meal that's perfect for a weeknight dinner or a casual dinner party. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
